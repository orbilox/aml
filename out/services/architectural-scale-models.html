Our Scale Model Making Process

A structured, detail-driven approach to deliver precise and high-quality architectural scale models.

1

Concept & Design Planning

Review architectural drawings and references. Define model purpose. Finalize detailing level and delivery timeline.

2

Scale Selection & Material Planning

Select the appropriate scale for the model. Choose materials like acrylic, wood, metal, or resin. Plan durability and display requirements.

3

Precision Model Making

Accurate measurements and proportional execution. Professional scale model builders ensure precision. Handcrafted and tool-assisted construction.

4

Detailing, Lighting & Finishing

Landscape, textures, and surface detailing. Optional LED lighting integration. Color matching and finish enhancement.

5

Quality Check & Delivery

Accuracy and finish inspection. Secure packaging for safe transport. Ready-to-display model delivery.
